How It Started

Born from a Passion for Horology

In 2019, three watch enthusiasts in Mumbai decided they were tired of paying import margins and settling for inaccessible luxury. They had one mission: make the world's most beautiful timepieces available to every Indian who appreciated fine craftsmanship.

Starting with just 12 carefully selected watches in a shared apartment, CHRONOS grew through word-of-mouth, obsessive customer service, and an unwavering commitment to authenticity.

"A watch is not just a tool that tells time. It's a daily reminder of your values, your taste, and what you've achieved."

Today, CHRONOS serves over 12,000 customers across India with 500+ models from luxury Swiss and Japanese makers — all 100% authentic, all beautifully curated.
