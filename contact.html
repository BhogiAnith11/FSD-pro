Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How do I cancel my order?+
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters processing. Email us at support@chronos.com with your Order ID immediately for assistance.
Do you offer COD (Cash on Delivery)?+
Yes! Cash on Delivery is available for orders up to ₹15,000. For orders above this amount, prepaid payment (card, UPI, or bank transfer) is required for security reasons.
Are all your watches 100% authentic?+
Absolutely. Every watch sold on CHRONOS is 100% authentic and comes with brand warranty. We source directly from authorized distributors and never deal in replicas or refurbished stock.
Can I place a bulk or corporate order?+
Yes! For bulk orders of 5 or more watches (corporate gifts, events, etc.), we offer special pricing and personalized engraving. Email corporate@chronos.com with your requirements.
Do you ship internationally?+
Currently we ship only within India. International shipping is coming soon. If you're an NRI interested in gifting, contact us and we'll try to accommodate your request.
How do I get a gift receipt or invoice?+
A digital invoice (GST compliant) is automatically emailed after every purchase. For a physical gift receipt without price, mention "gift" in the order notes at checkout or email us after placing the order.
