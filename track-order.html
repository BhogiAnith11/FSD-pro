Tracking FAQs

Where can I find my Order ID? +
Your Order ID is sent to your registered email and phone number via SMS immediately after placing your order. It starts with "CHR" followed by 8 digits (e.g. CHR12345678).
How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days. Express delivery (available at checkout) delivers within 2–3 business days. Metro cities typically receive orders faster.
My tracking hasn't updated in 2 days. What should I do? +
Tracking updates may be delayed by 24–48 hours during transit between cities. If there's no update for more than 3 days, please contact our support team at support@chronos.com with your Order ID.
Can I change the delivery address after ordering? +
Address changes are possible within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately at support@chronos.com.
I wasn't home during delivery. What happens? +
The courier will attempt delivery 3 times over 3 days. If all attempts fail, the package is returned to our warehouse and a full refund is processed within 5–7 business days.
