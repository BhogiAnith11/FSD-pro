Return FAQs

How long does the refund take after return pickup? +
Once we receive the returned watch, our quality team inspects it within 2–3 business days. After approval, the refund is processed and credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
Can I exchange for a more expensive watch? +
Yes! You can exchange for any watch in our catalog. If the new watch is more expensive, you pay the difference. If it's less expensive, we refund the balance to your original payment method.
What if I lost the original box or tags? +
Original packaging is preferred but not always mandatory. However, missing accessories, straps, or documentation may result in a deduction from your refund amount. Contact us first and we'll guide you.
My watch was a gift. Can it be returned? +
Yes! Gifted watches can be returned within 30 days. You'll need the original Order ID from the purchaser. The refund will be issued to the original buyer's payment method.
Is the return shipping really free? +
Absolutely! For all eligible returns and exchanges, we arrange a free doorstep pickup through our courier partner. You will never have to pay any return shipping charges.
