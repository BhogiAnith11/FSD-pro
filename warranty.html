Warranty FAQs

Does my warranty transfer if I gift the watch?+
Standard and Extended warranties are non-transferable. The Lifetime Warranty on Ultra Luxury watches is fully transferable to a new owner with proper documentation from CHRONOS.
What voids my warranty?+
Warranty is void if: the watch is repaired/opened by an unauthorized service center, intentional damage is evident, serial number is tampered with, or the watch is used beyond its stated water resistance rating.
How long does a warranty repair take?+
Standard repairs take 7–14 business days. Complex movement repairs may take up to 21 days. Extended and Lifetime warranty holders get priority processing with a guaranteed 10-day turnaround.
Can I extend my Standard 2-year warranty?+
Yes! You can upgrade to an Extended 5-year warranty for eligible watches within the first 6 months of purchase. Contact our support team for pricing and eligibility details.
Is the warranty valid across India?+
Yes, CHRONOS warranty is valid pan-India. We have authorized service centers in all major cities. For remote locations, we offer free courier pickup and delivery for warranty repairs.
